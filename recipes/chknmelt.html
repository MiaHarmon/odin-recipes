Chipotle Chicken Avocado Melt

A Cheap Recreation of the Panera Sandwich

This simple recipe no longer leaves you craving!

Ingredients

  • Shredded Chicken
  • Roasted Red Peppers
  • Avocado
  • Bread (Focaccia, Sourdough...)
  • Sliced Gouda

Sauce Ingredients

  • Mayo
  • Can of Chipotle Peppers in Adobo Sauce

Instructions

  1. Mince chipotle peppers, DO NOT throw away anything from the can
  2. Combine minced peppers with mayo and remaining sauce in the can until desired taste
  3. Slice avocado and set aside
  4. Using the broil setting on your oven, toast two slices of bread on one side
  5. Flip bread so the untoasted side is facing up and add chicken and gouda
  6. Put back into the broiler until cheese has melted
  7. Take out of oven and add the rest of the ingredients to the sandwich
